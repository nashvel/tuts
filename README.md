# Windsurf Account Creation Guide

This guide explains how to create multiple Windsurf accounts using temporary emails for testing or practice purposes.

---

## 1. Use Incognito Mode
Open your browser in **Incognito/Private mode** to ensure each registration starts with a fresh session.

---

## 2. Go to the Registration Page
Navigate to:  
**https://windsurf.com/account/register**

---

## 3. Fill in the Required Information
Enter your **Name** and **Email**.

Use a temporary email service such as:  
**https://tempmailo.com/**

- Copy the generated email and paste it into the Windsurf registration form.

---

## 4. Refresh Email for Each Account
When creating multiple accounts, click the **“Change”** button on TempMailo to generate a new email address for each new registration.

---

## 5. Complete the Signup Process
Continue with the registration flow.

When additional steps appear, press **“Skip”** until you reach the **pricing page**.

---

## 6. Access the Dashboard
Once you are on the pricing page:

1. Replace the URL with:  
   **https://windsurf.com/account/register**
2. Wait for it to automatically load into the Windsurf **dashboard**.

---

## 7. Log Out
Find and click the **Logout** button in the dashboard.

---

## 8. Repeat
To create another account:

1. Generate a new email using TempMailo (`Change` button).
2. Reopen the registration page in Incognito mode.
3. Repeat the steps above.
